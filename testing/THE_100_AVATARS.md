# ClinicIQ — The 100 Avatars
## Organized by tier, category, and specialty. Reviewable at a glance. Expandable to thousands.

---

## HOW THE 100 ARE ORGANIZED

**5 tiers × 20 avatars each = 100 total**

- **Tier 1: Celebrity Experts (10 avatars)** — Real-world inspired. Household names. 1M+ followers. NYT bestselling authors. These are the Josh Axes and Mark Hymans. We build their personas from public knowledge.
- **Tier 2: Established Leaders (15 avatars)** — 100K-1M followers. Published authors. Speaking circuits. Known in their niche but not mainstream household names. Running $2M+ practices or program businesses.
- **Tier 3: Growing Experts (25 avatars)** — 10K-100K followers. Have a program and some proof. Making $300K-$2M. Know their niche, need marketing systems. The sweet spot of ClinicIQ's ideal customer.
- **Tier 4: Early-Stage Practitioners (25 avatars)** — Under 10K followers. 1-5 years in practice. Have clinical skills but no marketing infrastructure. Some proof but not organized. The "I need help" tier.
- **Tier 5: Edge Cases & Stress Tests (25 avatars)** — Designed to break things. Multi-hyphenates, hostile personalities, niche specialties, brand-new with nothing, contradictory answers, wildly verbose.
