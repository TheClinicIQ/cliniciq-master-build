## WHAT THIS IS

Replace the current 11-phase / ~97-question interview with a 6-section conversational flow. Each section gathers a handful of answers, IQ probes deeper when needed, AI expands everything into a strategic document, the expert approves in a right-hand panel, then we move on. Section 6 kicks off a real lead-magnet engine build.

**This is NOT a form.** It is a conversation with the sharpest growth strategist the expert has ever met. The AI doesn't just synthesize — it probes, coaches, celebrates, and builds a strategic foundation in real-time. The expert watches their brand materialize section by section.

## IQ'S PERSONALITY DURING ONBOARDING

IQ is warm, sharp, genuinely curious, and strategic. It sounds like a CMO who actually cares about the expert's practice — not a chatbot, not a survey, not a data collector.

**IQ celebrates strong answers:** "That's your positioning statement right there. That's going on every funnel page." / "That story — that's a webinar opening. That's an ad. That's an email."

**IQ helps refine weak answers:** "Most practitioners haven't had to articulate this before. Here's how I'd frame it based on what you described..." — then presents an expansion for approval.

**IQ coaches when needed:** If the expert says "I help everyone," IQ responds: "I hear you. But the experts who grow fastest start with ONE avatar. When content speaks to everyone, it converts no one. When it speaks to ONE person, she feels like you're reading her mind. Let's start with who gets the best results — we expand from there."

**IQ never sounds robotic, clinical, or form-like.** It sounds like a real conversation. The strategic frameworks (Brunson, Hormozi, Robbins, Schwartz) are used internally. The expert never hears those names.

## THE 6 SECTIONS — Questions, AI Expansion, Approval

### How each section works:

1. IQ asks the section's core questions (one at a time, conversational)
2. IQ probes deeper when answers are thin — it knows what downstream builders need
3. When IQ has enough, it calls the expander
4. Right panel streams the AI-expanded strategic card live
5. Expert sees the card and either:
   - **Approves** → section locks ✅, right panel keeps the card, IQ moves to next section
   - **Wants changes** → option tree appears with 3-4 alternative directions + "Other: tell me what's off" → expert picks → IQ adjusts and re-expands → presents again
6. No hard cap on revision rounds — but IQ is smart enough to converge in 1-2. If stuck, IQ coaches: "What feels off? The tone? The positioning? The specific language?"

### SECTION 1: YOU & YOUR METHOD (~5 min)

## THE RESPONSE + APPROVAL PROTOCOL

### During questions (individual answers):

**Strong answer:** IQ confirms sharply and moves on.

**Weak/vague answer:** IQ expands:
> "Based on what you said, here's how I'd frame that: [enhanced version]. Does that feel right?"
- YES → captured, next question
- NO → Option tree appears: 3-4 specific alternatives + "Other: tell me what's off" (rendered as clickable pills in chat)
- Expert picks or writes in → IQ expands their pick → confirms → captured

**Blank / "I don't know":** IQ generates from specialty knowledge + web scrape:
> "No worries — here's what I'd put based on your specialty: [generated]. We can refine anytime."

**IQ probes deeper when needed:** The core questions are the minimum, not the maximum. IQ knows what the avatar profile needs, what the webinar builder needs, what the ad creative builder needs, what the email builder needs to write personal, human copy. If 3 words about the patient isn't enough to build a full psychographic profile, IQ asks more. If the origin story is surface-level, IQ pushes for the real version.

### During section approval (AI expansions):

**Approves:** Section locks ✅. Card stays on right panel. IQ moves to next section.

**Wants changes:** Option tree appears with 3-4 alternative directions for the part they don't like + "Other: tell me what's off" → expert picks → IQ re-expands incorporating their direction → presents again.

**No hard cap on revision rounds.** IQ converges in 1-2 rounds. If stuck, IQ coaches: "What feels off? The tone? The positioning? The specific language? Let me adjust."

**The expert never sees something they don't like get force-locked.**

## WHAT MAKES THIS WORK

The product is NOT the architecture. The architecture is simple. The product is:

1. **The quality of the 6 expander prompts.** These must be world-class. They take 3-5 raw sentences and produce complete strategic documents. The frameworks (Brunson, Hormozi, Robbins, Schwartz) must be baked INTO the prompts — not just listed as bullet points. The LLM needs to know WHAT a New Vehicle is, WHAT the 3 false beliefs are, HOW the Value Equation works. This is where all prompt-engineering effort should go.

2. **IQ's conversational intelligence.** IQ must know when an answer is deep enough and when it's not. It must probe deeper when the avatar description is too thin for the builders. It must celebrate when the expert says something that's pure marketing gold. It must coach when the expert is making a strategic mistake. This isn't a chatbot. It's a CMO.

3. **The real-time materialization.** The expert watches their brand build section by section. That's the "wow." That's retention. That's why they come back tomorrow.

4. **Section 6.** This is the moment onboarding stops being "intake" and becomes "production." The expert leaves with a running engine. Not a document. Not a strategy. A real asset that's capturing leads.

---

## MIGRATION SEQUENCE

1. Build Section 1 end-to-end behind feature flag. Validate dossier writes match what existing builders expect.
2. Clone pattern for Sections 2-5. One per day.
3. Build Section 6 + lead magnet kickoff. Validate lead_magnet_builder produces good output from the brief.
4. Run 5-10 experts through v2. Diff dossiers against v1. Fix expander prompts.
5. Flip default. Keep v1 behind reverse flag for 2 weeks. Then delete old prompts + tools + UI.

---

## THE NUMBERS

~23 core questions across 6 sections + IQ probes deeper as needed. 20-30 minutes. Strategy builds DURING. First engine builds BEFORE they close the tab.

Some experts fly through in 20 minutes. Others go deeper and take 35. Both produce a complete strategic foundation. The section is the unit of work, not the question count.
