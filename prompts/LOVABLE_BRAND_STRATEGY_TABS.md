## DESIGN NOTES FOR BOTH TABS

- All cards are white (#FFFFFF) on the #F8F9FA panel background. Subtle border #E5E7EB. Rounded-xl (12px).
- Section headers use small uppercase gray text (#6B7280) with an icon.
- Status badges: emerald for positive/active/locked, amber for in-progress/warning, red for alerts, gray for inactive.
- Every section has an action link (Edit, View, Apply, Add) in emerald — these are how the expert interacts with their brand and strategy.
- The Brand tab is more "profile-like" — showing who they are. The Strategy tab is more "operational" — showing what's happening this month.
- Both tabs should scroll vertically within the right panel. Content should be dense but well-spaced — no excessive whitespace, but every section breathes.
- The cards should feel alive with real data — specific numbers, specific phrases, specific names. Nothing generic. Everything is Dr. Sarah Chen's actual brand.
