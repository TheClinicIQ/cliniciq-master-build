# ClinicIQ — Streamlined Onboarding System
## 6 Sections. ~20 Questions. AI Expands Everything. Expert Approves.
### The Tesla Ordering Model: Minimum clicks, maximum output.

---

## THE PRINCIPLE

The expert provides the raw seed — a few sentences about their practice, their patients, their story. IQ instantly expands that seed into a comprehensive strategic document for that section. The expert reads it, approves it, edits it, or says "not quite" and IQ adjusts. Then we move to the next section.

**Old model:** 103 questions → expert talks for 45-75 minutes → platform builds strategy after
**New model:** 6 sections × 3-5 questions each → AI expands after each section → expert approves in real-time → done in 20-30 minutes → strategy is already built by the time they finish

The expert should feel like they're having a short, sharp conversation — and watching their entire brand and strategy materialize in front of them in the right panel as they go.

- New content created on schedule (daily/weekly — whatever cadence was set)

## THE WEAK RESPONSE PROTOCOL (still applies — simplified)

Same 4 levels but FASTER because AI is doing most of the work:

**Strong answer:** Confirm. AI expands. Expert approves. Move on.

**Weak answer:** AI expands anyway using specialty knowledge + web scrape data. Presents the expansion. "Here's what I'd build from that — does this feel right?"
- YES → locked, move on
- NO → IQ adjusts based on their feedback. One round. Then locked.

**Blank:** AI generates the entire section from specialty defaults + web scrape. "Based on what I know about your specialty, here's what I'd put. We can refine anytime."

**The conversation never stalls.** 22 questions. 20-30 minutes. Strategy built. Engine started. Done.

## WHAT AI EXPANDS FROM EACH SECTION (the invisible intelligence)

The expert gives seeds. AI applies these frameworks invisibly:

**From Section 1 (Method):** → Brunson New Vehicle framing, Character Type, Epiphany Bridge structure, contrarian positioning, enemy identification, why-now urgency

**From Section 2 (Patient):** → 4-layer avatar psychographics, Schwartz awareness level, Robbins 6 needs ranking, 3 false beliefs (Brunson), buying triggers, internal dialogue, patient failure language (for ad hooks)

**From Section 3 (Offers):** → Hormozi Value Equation scoring, friction inventory, guarantee framework, bonus ideation, offer stack transformation framing, before/after state, denominator optimization

**From Section 4 (Proof):** → Proof classification (which belief each piece breaks), proof deployment map, gap analysis, capture suggestions

**From Section 5 (Voice):** → Full Voice DNA profile, content tone rules, ALWAYS/NEVER messaging rules, brand personality framework

**From Section 6 (Lead Magnet):** → Lead magnet spec, opt-in page copy, funnel architecture, email sequence themes, content calendar seed, social post hooks

All of this generates the 7 strategy documents in the background. By the time onboarding is done, the strategy exists. The expert never had to think about strategy. They just told IQ about themselves.
