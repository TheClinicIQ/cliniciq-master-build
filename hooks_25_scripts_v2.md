# ClinicIQ — Hooks for All 25 Video Scripts (V2)
## Facebook & Instagram · Text Overlay + Verbal Hook

**The real rules:**
- Fear is the scroll-stopper. Not vague fear. Specific, personal, "that's me" fear.
- The written overlay must identify them clearly — health expert, practitioner, clinician — so they know immediately this is for them
- 6 words or fewer on screen. Every word earns its place or it's cut.
- Verbal hook extends the fear and opens the loop — never closes it

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**What changed from V1 and why:**
Every hook now does three things V1 didn't:
1. **Identifies the viewer explicitly** — "clinicians," "practitioners," "health experts," "doctors" — so they know in the first half-second this is for them
2. **Names a specific fear** — not vague disruption, but a concrete personal consequence
3. **Makes the cost of inaction immediate** — not theoretical, not "someday" — happening right now, to them, today

**The fear hierarchy that drives click-through on FB/IG:**
1. Fear of irreversible loss (MA-02, MA-03, UA-04)
2. Fear of being wrong about something you were confident about (SA-01, SA-03, PA-04)
3. Fear of being left behind while unaware (UA-03, UA-05, SA-02)
4. Fear of wasted effort (PA-01, PA-05)
5. FOMO on something others are already getting (PR-01, MA-04)

**Priority test order:**
PA-02 → UA-03 → SA-01 → MA-05 → PA-03 → MA-01 → PR-03
