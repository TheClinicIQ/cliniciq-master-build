### The Teaching — Voice DNA

"Here's how it works technically.

It captures:
- Your **sentence rhythm** — do you write in short punchy bursts, or do you build long flowing arguments? Or do you mix them?
- Your **vocabulary register** — clinical terms? Plain language? Street-level casual?
